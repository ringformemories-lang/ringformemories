ABOUT

One ring for a lifetime of
memories

Ring for Memories provides vintage style audio guestbooks for weddings, birthdays, and special events. Your guests pick up the phone, leave a voice message, and you get to keep those recordings forever.

We handle delivery, setup, and collection; you just enjoy your day. After the event, all recorded messages are sent straight to you.
